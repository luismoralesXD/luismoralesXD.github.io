How To Use

Each click on a tab opens its own section

and must be pressed again to close the section.

Failure to do so will result in a long ongoing

collection of clicked sections until its tab has been

clicked again. No matter the order in which each tab

has been clicked, they will always rearrange in order.


For best results, use Desktop Chrome Broswer in full screen

Hollerith Tabulator

Gave better alternative to tabulate data.

Saved government time completeing the census.

Saved the government money.

Source Information: https://www.census.gov/history/www/innovations/technology/the_hollerith_tabulator.html

Turing Machine

Gave ideas which later based the central concept of the modern computer.

Lead to Turing-computability.

Capable of computing anything that is computable.

Source Information: https://plato.stanford.edu/entries/turing-machine/

Atanasoff Berry Computer

First computer to store Information in its main memory.

Base two numbers.

First electronic digital computer

Source Information: http://jva.cs.iastate.edu/operation.php
