Where to find job opportunites.

Job opportunites can be found either in person or online. If doing it in person, politely ask one of the workers to speak to the manager. If for whatever reason the manager is not available, either ask the worker directly for an application or ask for when the manager will be available and plan accordingly. Looking through newspapers can be another good way to find jobs. As well as talking to family members, friends, and neighbors for job opportunites.

Another good way to find a job is through online sites. Going to a specific company website and going to their career section is a good example. There are sites as well with lists of jobs to look through. A list with some of these sites can be found below...
